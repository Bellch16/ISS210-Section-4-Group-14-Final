A Call To Action

By Austin Waller

Posted on April 28, 2023

One way to support zoo animals is to go to accredited non-profit zoos, to support them on the individual level, and educate yourself about zoos. Refrain from visiting zoos that mistreat or under-nourish their animals. Support conservation efforts that aim to protect endangered species and their habitats. This can help to reduce the demand for captive breeding programs and other forms of captivity. Another way to help is by donating to or volunteering for the American Humane Society, where you can do your part to make a difference in an animals life, you can help ensure that zoos have to go through the proper certification, and you can help to ensure that all of the animals of earth can be saved.
